1. What is a cookie? 
Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device. They are widely used to make websites work or work better and more efficiently. They perform this function because websites can read and write these files, allowing to recognize the User and remember important information that will make more comfortable the use of a website (for example, remembering the preferred settings).
